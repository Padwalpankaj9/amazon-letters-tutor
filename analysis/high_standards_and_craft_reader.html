High Standards and Craft

High standards mean knowing what good looks like, knowing the true scope of the work, and caring about invisible quality.

For life

Use it for writing, health, school, work, and relationships by asking whether you understand the real standard and the effort required.

For business

Use it to build teams where quality is teachable, visible, and protected even when the work is behind the scenes.

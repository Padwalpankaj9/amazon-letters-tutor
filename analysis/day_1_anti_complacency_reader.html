Day 1 Anti-Complacency

Day 1 is the habit of staying urgent, curious, customer-close, and allergic to institutional drift after success arrives.

For life

Use it when a good routine has quietly turned into autopilot. Ask what reality, customer, or external trend you are no longer seeing.

For business

Use it to diagnose whether a team is defending yesterday's process or still learning from customers, trends, and fast decisions.
